Don't use openssl s_client ... for plugin - it doesn't verify certificates  
properly, and it is documented not to (it's a debug tool after all).

Do use fetchmail's native SSL support instead.

In your rcfile, include something like this for the account in question...

ssl sslcertck

and remove the plugin part.
