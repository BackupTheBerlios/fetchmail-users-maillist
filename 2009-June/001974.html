Further information:

As a general debugging aid (Rob, this one's for you :-)):

SSL and TLS failures get logged as "upgrade to TLS failed" or "SSL  
connection failed", followed by a /socket/ error.

This however is logged as authorization failure, and as such it is really  
a genuine authentication error unrelated to SSL/TLS - at least in recent  
6.3.n versions.
