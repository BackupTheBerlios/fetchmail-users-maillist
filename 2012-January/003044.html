Perhaps a real-world-ish explanation of how multidrop works (or might
work) will help.

Carol has a real email address, carol at example.com.  She goes to
http://notsharingmy.info/ and defines two aliases for her mail there.
5gwry at notsharingmy.info and 99mkk at notsharingmy.info.

She then sets up fetchmail to retrieve her maildrop at example.com and
lets it forward the 5gwry address to bob at example.com and 99mkk to
alice at example.com.  Her usual email for carol goes to her localhost and
ends up in her email client.

I hope that helped a bit.
