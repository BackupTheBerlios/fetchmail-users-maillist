>        As just stated, "my" fetchmail, which is
>        6.3.18+GSS+NTLM+SDPS+SSL+NLS+KRB5(installed when I

It only does that because you have an IMAP or POP server installed,
and even there it knows nothing about where those files are since that
is handled by your IMAP or POP server.

As Matthias said, you have to configure your IMAP or POP server to
retrieve those files and then (and only then) can Fetchmail access
them.

What happens to the mail after Fetchmail retrieves it depends on the
configuration of your chosen MTA or MDA.
