Alan,

you must be thinking of a different tool than the fetchmail that is
discussed on this list.  "My" fetchmail does not support reading from
traditional mail spools, unless you add a separate software that exports
the mail spool through IMAP, such as UW's imapd.  Fetchmail does not do
that by itself, neither for /var/mail/USER or /var/spool/mail/USER, nor
for $HOME/mail/new/.

If you are permitted to install software system-wide, and the message
storage format is Maildir (i. e. there are cur and tmp directories under
~/mail), or traditional /var(/spool)/mail, I recommend installing
Dovecot on the computer that needs to export the mail, and only then can
you use fetchmail on the computer that needs to download the mail.

I hope that helps.
