As documented in the man page, "no keep" says to delete the emails
from the remote server once downloaded.  Try changing that to "keep",
though you'll also need to enable UIDL (also documented in the man
page).

I am however surprised that anything's working given that you've
configured it to send your email to the Google POP3 server.  I would
strongly suggest you remove the smtphost entry.
