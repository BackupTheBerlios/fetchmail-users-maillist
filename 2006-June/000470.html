Well, some options not yet mentioned are (without regard to their
usability):

 - dedicated resource tracking with a specific list

 - store the address list in one of the per-server or per-poll
   structures, rather than globally

 - avoid the crude longjmp() (which should've been siglongjmp anyways)
   but rely on EINTR or similar.

 - give up on SIGALRM and use non-blocking I/O and select() instead.

I haven't yet checked which way I'll go though.
