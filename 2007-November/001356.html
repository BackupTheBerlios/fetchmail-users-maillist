David,

the message is misleading - sorry for that.

Fetchmail has fetched the message, thereby had the chance of finding out
there was a length mismatch (between what the server claimed beforehand
and what fetchmail actually got trying), delivered it, and not flushed it.

Why fetchmail hasn't flushed the message I don't know, but it's likely a
matter of your setup, and not caused by the size mismatch.
