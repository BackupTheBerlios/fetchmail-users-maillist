> Fetchmail users,
>
> Here Fetchmail runs in Debian Squeeze
> along with Xfce.
>
> Two or three times per day, the system
> freezes.  The only recovery I've found
> is power cycling.  After the reboot,
> I invoke fetchmail from the command line
> and get a message that a stale lockfile
> is being removed.
>
> I haven't observed the freeze in absence
> of the Xfce GUI.
>
> Any insights about this?

1) Your problem is not related to fetchmail

2) Fetchmail is telling you that it failed to shut down cleanly, but
is handling the situation correctly

3) You may have either a hardware or a package problem

I'd suggest you try a different GUI (Gnome/KDE/whatever) and then if
that doesn't resolve your problem take a look at the drivers and
hardware you're using.  Any future problems should be directed to a
relevant Debian facility (list, forum etc) as it's obvious (to me)
that it's nothing to do with fetchmail.
