Am 09.04.2009, 19:02 Uhr, schrieb Gerard <gerard at seibercom.net>:

> I have been asked my opinion regarding setting up fetchmail to retrieve
> mail from several GMail IMAP accounts.
>
> Can anyone tell me specifically, what if any problems I might
> encounter? Is GMail's IMAP server fully RFC compliant? If not, what
> problems exist.

To the best of my knowledge, GMail is not fully RFC compliant and does  
strange things with labels, folders, and deletion.
I cannot and will not support use of fetchmail with GMail where GMail  
deviates from the RFC, but will fix genuine fetchmail bugs.

I'm afraid you will have to look through the list archives to find details  
of known GMail issues.
