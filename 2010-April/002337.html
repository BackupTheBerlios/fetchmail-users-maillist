This isn't a fetchmail question, but I'll answer nonetheless,

you do that with a proper mail application on your Red Hat Linux that
understands such attachments.  Try Thunderbird for an easy to use mailer and
OpenOffice.org for a spreadsheet application.

Note that fetchmail is only supported on operating systems that are supported by
their respective vendor.  If your Red Hat is very old (7.2 for instance is) and
out of vendor support, you're on your own.
