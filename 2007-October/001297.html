Ruan schrieb:
> Hi
> 
> I've got fetchmail set up properly and it works like a dream!
> However, I would like to rewrite the from address based on the subject 
> of the e-mail before fetchmail sends it to port 25 on my server. I.e:
> 
> Incoming message:
> 
> From: user1 at somewhere.com
> Subject: 12345
> 
> Fetchmail retrieves and injects back to:
> 
> From: 12345 at somewhere.com
> To: someuser at localhost
> 
> Is this possible?

Most MTAs have address rewriting facilities. For instance, Postfix,
Sendmail and Exim can do that. Else you can use the --mda option, script
something about formail or reformail to rewrite headers, or use Perl, and
then reinject.

HTH
Matthias
