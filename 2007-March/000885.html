Right, please go back and read the FAQ.  It tells you what you're
supposed to be providing.

1) Version of fetchmail.  Until that's (at least) 6.3.7 don't bother
going any further.

2) Contents of .fetchmailrc

3) Command line arguments

4) Output of "--nosyslog --nodetach -vvv" (yes, that's three -v
options, enabling debug mode) for a problem email

5) Matching entries from the log of your SMTP server (and what it is)

See also the FAQ:

http://www.fetchmail.info/fetchmail-FAQ.html#M8
