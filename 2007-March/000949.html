The logs strongly suggest you have some form of sendmail problem, not
fetchmail:

> fetchmail: SMTP> MAIL FROM:<B.Kramer at nutricia.com> SIZE=605
> fetchmail: SMTP< 501 Sender domain must exist
> fetchmail: SMTP error: 501 Sender domain must exist

So, your sendmail server isn't recognising nutricia.com as a valid
domain in DNS.  That you can send mail *TO* this just shows that
sendmail accepts mail *FOR* it.

You need to ensure that if you do "nslookup nutricia.com" resolves on
your sendmail server.

As was said, this is nothing to do with fetchmail - it's purely a
DNS/sendmail problem.
