John <fetchmail at jelmail.com> writes:

> Is there an extension to Fetchmail that allows users' email account 
> details to be stored in their LDAP user profile and extracted by 
> Fetchmail rather than storing them in a fetchmailrc file ?

Not to my knowledge. You'd have to secure the LDAP database (or at least
the password records) tightly, because you'd have to put cleartext
passwords into the database -- unless you were trying certificate-based
authentication, that is.
