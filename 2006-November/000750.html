But why the superfluous CAPA? I know the server does not support SSL or
TLS, and I have no "ssl" for it in my fetchmailrc, so why look for it at
all? It probably does not harm much, but I think we should not bother
the server unnecessarily, so the extra connect should be avoided. We
could instead try to login despite of the failed CAPA, and only
reconnect after this login failed (which will surely happen with some
servers).
