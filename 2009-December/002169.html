> I determined that this is caused by the inclusion of AI_ADDRCONFIG in the
> ai_flags passed to getaddrinfo().  Under this OS, AI_ADDRCONFIG is allowed as a

> both of the 
> #ifdef AI_ADDRCONFIG

> #if defined(AI_ADDRCONFIG) && !defined(_SCO_DS)
