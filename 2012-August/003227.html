On Thu 16 Aug 2012 23:47:11 NZST +1200, Gavin Henry wrote:

> I'm not sure why, but running fetchmail via cron has resolved the
> issue. It may just be a coincidence though.

The main difference between running from your console shell and running
fro cron is the environment, which in the case of cron is severely
stripped down. Replace fetchmail with a wrapper script that logs the
environment (env >someplace) before calling fetchmail and compare.

The other possibility may be that the cloud servers you got for some
some period weren't working the same, and/or as they should. In that
case the coincidence may bite you again.
