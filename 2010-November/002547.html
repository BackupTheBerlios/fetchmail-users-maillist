Wow! That's some .fetchmailrc file. I have used this for years with
success:


poll "pop.west.cox.net"
protocol pop3                   # we use the POP-protocol
username "xxxxxx"               # who am i ?
password "xxxxxxxxxx"           # passwords are always secret ;-)
mimedecode                      # automaticaly deal with mime
(attachements)
mda "/usr/bin/procmail -f -"    # tell fetchmail which MDA to use
 
It appears the problem is with your filters. What do your logs say? You
have verbose logging turned on but don't say what it showed.
 
You would get better answers from the procmail list:  

http://mailman.rwth-aachen.de/mailman/listinfo/procmail

BTW, I have run procmail for years with complete satisaction. I know
some people don't like it and advocate maildrop. To each his own.
