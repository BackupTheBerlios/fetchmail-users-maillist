> I have a problem, the fetchmail not marking mail as read after fetching.

IMAP uses client side tracking of what is read and what isn't, which
means that your other mail client has no way of knowing that fetchmail
has "read" the message.  You could try POP3, with both UIDL and KEEP
options set (from memory).
