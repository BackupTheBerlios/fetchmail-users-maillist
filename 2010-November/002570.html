> > Hi,

> > I have a problem, the fetchmail not marking mail as read after fetching.
> 
> IMAP uses client side tracking of what is read and what isn't, which
> means that your other mail client has no way of knowing that fetchmail
> has "read" the message.  You could try POP3, with both UIDL and KEEP
> options set (from memory).

Actually, IMAP does store it on the server, using the "SEEN" flag.
POP doesn't, either it's on the server or it isn't.
With POP, it's up to the client to remember if a message has been seen yet or 
not.

This is why I prefer IMAP over POP as well, I can use webmail to access my 
email and when I read a message, my desktop mail-client already knows I have 
read the message.
