DO NOT DO THAT!

That's the best way to DESTROY log lines. fetchmail does not release the  
file descriptor until terminated, so you'll be appending your logs to a  
deleted file, which is no longer accessible except to the running  
fetchmail process - and once that goes away, so will the file.

There's a reason why I wrote that fetchmail needs to be stopped. I wish  
the know-it-alls could keep their smattering to themselves.
