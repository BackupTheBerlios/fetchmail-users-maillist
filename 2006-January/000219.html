Probably the same as <https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=231607>.

Try this on your fetchmail 6.3.1 source directory (requires Perl installed):

perl -ple 's/([AP])F_UNSPEC/\1F_INET/g' -i.bak \
     checkalias.c driver.c env.c servport.c

Then rebuild and reinstall fetchmail 6.3.1 and let us know if the
problem is gone.
