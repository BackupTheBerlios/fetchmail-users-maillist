If you do not "set syslog", but "set logfile" /var/log/fetchmail.log, yes.
If your syslogd is configured /etc/syslog.conf to redirect syslogs there,
yes. Else typical places are /var/log/mail.info, /var/log/mail,
/var/log/maillog, depending on OS.

/etc/syslog.conf usually has the locations, which see.
