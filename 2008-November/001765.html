Well, since you aren't using the latest version of fetchmail, and you've
paid Novell to support for your older fetchmail version, I'm afraid I
cannot help you.

Problems with "10 messages" however rather hint to networking problems or
problems on either the upstream POP3/IMAP or the downstream SMTP/LMTP
servers or MDA, than a problem inside fetchmail.

Running fetchmail -vvv --nosyslog --nodetach may help identify issues.

Now, your OES is still covered by Novell general support until end July
2009, so please see http://support.novell.com/ and your support agreements
with Novell for your options.

Bottlenecks inside fetchmail do exist, but shouldn't become visible before
keeping thousands of messages in the upstream server's (POP3) mailbox.

Sorry.
