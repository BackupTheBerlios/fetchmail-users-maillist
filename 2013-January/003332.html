> The order of processing depends on how you've configured fetchmail.
> The only thing that's certain is that fetchmail retrieves the emails

Michael actually posted some material to the maildrop list, and it was
archived (as thread) at (through the awkward and unwieldy forums interface):

<http://sourceforge.net/mailarchive/forum.php?thread_name=50987.120.148.195.2.1359265602.squirrel%40www.holisticgp.com.au&forum_name=courier-maildrop>

And Michael will either have to

- tell Postfix (through mailbox_transport or mailbox_command, or a
.forward rule) to route message through maildrop

or, alternatively,

- tell fetchmail to invoke maildrop as MDA.

If dovecot-lda were to come into play, it would then be called from
~/.mailfilter.
