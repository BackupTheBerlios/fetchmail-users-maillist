> I am seeking advice on the overall process with *postfix* and *dovecot*
> when fetchmail is configured to download mail, and then hand it over to
> maildrop for delivery via the ../bin/mda command. I know that if postfix
> is stopped, fetchmail complains that it can't deliver to the localhost. So
> does this mean that postfix and dovecot are serving as middlemen here? I

Without your fetchmail configuration file (fetchmailrc), any command
line options you're using *and* the actual log entries/errors in
question people are reduced to guessing. Please provide all of that
and the chances are your problem will be easily solved.

> I'm not seeking a specific explanation at this stage as I am not certain
> my problem is fetchmail related. I just what to better understand the
> process and the order of processing.

The order of processing depends on how you've configured fetchmail.
The only thing that's certain is that fetchmail retrieves the emails
from an IMAP, POP3 or other server and hands them over to *something*
for further processing. What that is, and what it does, are up to you.
