> Any suggestions on the best way to deal with this kind of header ?


I've given this some thought, and here's my suggestion.

Allow the envelope string to be interpreted as a Posix extended regex if it begins with ^. So:

envelope "^X-Apparently-To: +([A-Za-z0-9._%+-]+ at yahoo.com) "

The caret ^ not only indicates the presence of the regex, but also anchors the match
to the beginning of the string.

The first substring identifies the matched address.
