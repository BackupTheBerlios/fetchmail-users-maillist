Assuming your local POP3/IMAP4 server supports this then yes.

If from the command line you email account1 and then account 2, does
each go to the right account?  What is the output of the command in
step 7 of FAQ entry G3
(http://www.fetchmail.info/fetchmail-FAQ.html#G3) for an email that
goes to the "wrong" place?  What shows up in your mail server's logs
for the same email?

Your .fetchmailrc looks correct for when you're polling multiple
accounts, and is pretty much exactly what I (and many others) do
(though your final "smtphost localhost" line isn't required).
