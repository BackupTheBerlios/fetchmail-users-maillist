Chris Worley schrieb:
> I'm using Fetchmail over IMAP to gather my email to my laptop, but, I want
> to keep the email on the server so my mobile gets my email too.

 > But, if I read my email on my mobile before fetchmail fetches it, then 
it > is marked as read, and fetchmail won't fetch it... and I have no complete
 > history of my email on my laptop.

Hi Chris,

I'm sorry to say you've indeed bumped into a missing feature of 
fetchmail's, which doesn't yet do (in 6.3.X) client-side UID tracking for 
IMAP - which is the same model as --uidl is for POP3 - with one important 
difference (UIDVALIDITY).

UID support for IMAP4 is a feature that is on my TODO list, and would 
enable you to do exactly what you desire.

If you have POP3 access to the same mailbox, try --keep --uidl.

HTH
Matthias
