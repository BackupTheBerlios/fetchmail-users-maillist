That's it!! you rock!

Here is the script I am using:

#!/usr/bin/perl -w
$dumpDir="/home/michael/fetchmail/dump";

@timeData = gmtime(time);
$myFileName= join('', @timeData);
$range = 1000;
$random_number = int(rand($range));
$myFileName = $myFileName . "_1" . $random_number . ".eml";
open(FILE, ">>$dumpDir/$myFileName") || die "Cannot open my_file: $!\n";
while(<STDIN>)
{
chomp;
    print FILE $_ . "\r\n";
}
close(FILE);
exit;

seems to work :)
