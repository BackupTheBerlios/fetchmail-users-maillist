i think i solved the problem on my own: i used the '-a' option in my
local fetchmail command:
/bin/su wolfgang -c '/usr/bin/fetchmail -a -f
/home/wolfgang/.fetchmailrc -d 300 -L /home/wolfgang/fetchmail.log 2>&1'
