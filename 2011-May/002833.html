> BTW. As I reported on the list after you sent me the
> FETCHMAIL_IMAP_DELETED_REMAINS_UNSEEN patch, although the patch did what
> it was supposed to do, it made things actually worse (for me) since
> exchange as a consequence sends "deleted without being read" receipts.

Yes, I've seen your findings and I have since updated the manual page
accordingly, from 6.3.20-pre1:

FETCHMAIL_IMAP_DELETED_REMAINS_UNSEEN
      (since v6.3.20): If this environment variable  is  set  and  not
      empty,  fetchmail  will NOT mark messages retrieved through IMAP
      as \Seen when they are deleted. This may suppress delivery noti‐
      fications  on  some  systems  (some versions of HP OpenMail) and
      change them to mention "deleted without being  read"  on  others
      (some  versions  of  Microsoft  Exchange).  The default (if this
      variable is unset or empty) is to mark  messages  as  \Seen  and
      \Deleted at the same time.

I am considering removing that patch for 6.3.20 again, or possibly move
it to contrib/.

Thanks again.
