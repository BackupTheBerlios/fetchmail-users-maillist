On Mon, Feb 15, 2010 at 12:53, Kārlis Repsons <karlis.repsons at gmail.com> wrote:
> Hi list,
> do I get it right, that its currently not possible to poll two or more
> accounts/servers in the same time? Well, in case I have 2+ accounts and would
> need to use IMAP IDLE, its no less than a blocker... Comments?

It is possible (and I do) as long as you're not using IDLE.  If you're
using IDLE then it's one daemon one IMAP account since fetchmail isn't
multi-threaded.  There's nothing stopping you running multiple
fetchmail daemons however, just ensure you use different pidfiles for
each.
