It's hard to tell what exactly went wrong. Generally, you have two
options (I'm not sure if webmin supports one, the other, or both - in
doubt, check with SUSE - you've got their commercial product after all).

- map the postmaster to an existing user at your site with Postfix aliases,
  check the Postfix configuration and manual

- configure fetchmail to send root messages to the right user:
  set postmaster "user at domain.example" (put this before the first "poll"
  stanza)
