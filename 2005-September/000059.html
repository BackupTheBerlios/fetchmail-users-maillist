Upgrade to 6.2.5.2 (or 6.3).  The version you're running has known

> fetchmail: socket error while fetching from mail.server.com 
> fetchmail: 6.2.5 querying mail.server.com ( protocol POP3) at <date and
> time>: poll completed 
> fetchmail Query status=2 (SOCKET) 

In short, as you'll know from reading the archive, SOCKET errors mean
that the network connection between fetchmail and either the SMTP or
POP/IMAP server failed.  Often you can get details from "fetchmail -v
-v" or from looking in your mail log that'll tell you what failed.
