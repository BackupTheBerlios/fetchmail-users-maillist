Forgot to add :

Whatever strategy you use, make sure that the destination of fetchmail --
be it an MTA or amavisd-new -- is configured to *never* reject email from
fetchmail; it may be configured to shunt spam to a certain mailbox, or drop
the message to the floor silently, but *never* reject them outright.

Failure to do so will result in fetchmail looping again and again trying to
deliver the rejected email.
