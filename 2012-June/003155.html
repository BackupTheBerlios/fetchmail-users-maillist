What the POP3 server marks seen or not is its own business and nothing
fetchmail has reliable control over. Trying -U (aka --uidl) is worthwhile.

I suggest that you try "proto POP3 UIDL interval 3" and, unrelated to
your issue, possibly add "sslcertck" after ssl.

Given your configuration does not comprise "keep", messages are supposed
to be removed after downloading (but old messages will remain on the
server unless you use --flush), chances are this doesn't work properly.
Logs as requested below would help diagnosing the problem.

>> any hints?
