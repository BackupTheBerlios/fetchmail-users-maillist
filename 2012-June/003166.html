c_rehash must(!!!) match your version of OpenSSL, 0.9.X vs. 1.0.0, else
it will not work.

Converting the .p7b to .pem as described in my other mail and then
passing the .pem file as sslcertfile should work (at least it works with
gnutls-cli -p 995 --x509cafile RapidSSL.pem my.inbox.com).

You will not need sslcertpath then.
