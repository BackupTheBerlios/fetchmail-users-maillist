On Wed, May 28, 2008 at 3:22 PM, ram <ram at netcore.co.in> wrote:
> I have a mailbox with 30k mails.
> I want to enable pop on it and get all newer mail via fetchmail
>
> How do I configure fetchmail to ignore first 30k mails

If you enable UIDL fetchmail will keep a track of which messages it
has downloaded and only download messages it hasn't seen before (you
need to also use "keep" to avoid fetchmail deleting emails after it
has downloaded them).

Note that there isn't a simple way with POP to avoid fetchmail
initially downloading the entire mailbox to populate the list of
messages it has seen.
