Hi Rob,

I'd suggest to disable the Digest option for the list.

It usually causes more pain than it's worth. People wait for the digest,  
or use mailers incapable of replying to individual messages in the digest  
(causing meaningless Subject headers such as Re: fetchmail-users Vol 12   
Issue 345) and so forth.  Some mailers (Opera) automatically file mailing  
list mail into separate folders, with many others it's easy to filter --  
and the other setups can usually use maildrop, procmail or similar.

Are there relevant objections?
