Bryan Humphreys schrieb am 2009-05-16:

> I am looking for some help.
> 
> I want to use fetchmail to retrieve mail from a pop account and pass it
> to mailman.
> Do you have any configuration examples that I could use both for fetch
> mail and the config in mailman.
> 
> Thanking you all in advance
> Your help will be much appreciated

Don't do that. POP3 wasn't designed to support such operations, and I
personally will not be supporting people who have too little experience
with operating mail systems in doing such dangerous things. I can
already hear the "this doesn't work" and "that doesn't work"...

If you want to continue nonetheless, read
<http://www.fetchmail.info/fetchmail-man.html#41> - and if you then
still have questions, again the answer will be: don't do that.

Arrange for UUCP (that works without fetchmail), ODMR or ETRN or proper
SMTP feed (possibly through tunnels) if you want to operate mailing
lists.
