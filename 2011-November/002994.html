Well, it works for me in Ubuntu 11.04 amd64,
when leaving aside the facts that:

a) Ubuntu is shipping an outdated version of fetchmail, and its
changelog does not mention that they backported the fix for
CVE-2011-1947, so chances are it's unfixed and can hang, see
http://www.fetchmail.info/fetchmail-SA-2011-01.txt for details.

b) un- and reinstalling a package on dpkg-based systems does not
recreate configuration files -- for that, you'd have to save what you
want to preserve, and *purge* the package (i. e. delete it and its
configuration) and re-install it, then reinstall the saved configuration.
