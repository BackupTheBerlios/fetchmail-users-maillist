Which appears to be the case - the message-IDs are the same for all
three deliveries, and your fetchmail configuration isn't using
multidrop.  There is only one recipient address (brian, brian2, or
diane), and no wildcard.

The other source of such deliveries would be local aliases or forwards,
but we can preclude that, it would cause different logging from Exim.
