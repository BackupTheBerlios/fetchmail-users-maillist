On Sun, 6 Nov 2011, brian wrote:

> I want to do the following though. When an email comes in I want to look at 
> the sender and the subject
> and trigger a script on my server if certain conditions are met. Its going to 
> stay pretty basic.

   I do that (and more), with the following line in .fetchmailrc

           mda '/home/frenkiel/bin/mail_filter' antispam -1 mimedecode

For the basic things you want to do, you can use something like that:

    TMP=/tmp/filter_$$.tmp
    cat > $TMP
    FROMLINE=`grep ^From: $TMP | head -1`
    SUBJ=`grep ^Subject: $TMP | head -1`

    and then set your conditions with
    case $FROMLINE in
    . . .

    case $SUBJ in ...
    . . .
