>- to _disable_ encryption: omit/remove "ssl", and use "sslproto ssl23"
>
>- to _enable_ wrapped SSL/TLS on separate ports (993/995):
>  use "ssl sslcertck sslproto ssl23"
>
>- to _enable_ STARTTLS on the "normal" ports (143/110):
>  use "sslcertck sslproto tls1", omit/remove "ssl".
