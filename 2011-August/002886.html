Postfix, your SMTP server is rejecting the email because the email address 
provided is: "Martina at pdpfqtcgpdqyciphqeggp.{SPF_D1}"

This will not be accepted by default Postfix configuration as the email 
address is wrong.

I would like to suggest you try to get a copy of the actual email, including 
all the headers.
    (Matthias, can fetchmail write the emails to flatfiles?)

My guess, based on the address, is that it could be spam.
Do you know a Martina? If yes, ask her what she sent and if she could try 
resending it?
