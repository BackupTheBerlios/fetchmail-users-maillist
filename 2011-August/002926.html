Turned out this was a fetchmail bug in versions [6.3.5; 6.3.20]
(including both). It was triggered by an incomplete fix to remove
trailing closing parentheses in messages with unterminated (no CRLF/LF)
last line that got downloaded through IMAP.

It is fixed in fetchmail 6.3.21 (see separate announcement), but not yet
in the 7-alpha releases.
