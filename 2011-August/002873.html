It sounds like a firewall/routing problem, but as you've chosen to
hide the real hostname we can only assume it's probably a firewall
problem. Have you tried talking to the IT support department at your
work?

> Is there a way to use fetchmail from a machine outside of the department network, thru this "host.dept.fr" e.g.?

Yes - many people do. However it requires that your IMAP or POP server
is accessible from outside your network, and that the hostname you're
connecting to resolves correctly. Talking to your IT support
department should help you.

Also, please read the FAQ, in particular the section on reporting problems.
