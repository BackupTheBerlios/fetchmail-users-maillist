Am 20.01.2010, 22:17 Uhr, schrieb Stefan Onken <support at stonki.de>:

> I am receiving some emails with incorrect header lines. Usually the
> whitespace before multiline header lines are broken (spamassassin
> messages). Fetchmail is actually dropping those emails complaining about
> an incorrect header. I googled for a solution and got stuck with a lot
> of problems but no solutions. Is there a way to force fetchmail to
> ignore those lines ?

Not unless you modify the source code and recompile.

If you change fetchmail like that, you're not gaining any guarantees that  
the mail is actually delivered, your MTA or MDA might still reject or drop  
it.

The best bet is to make sure that the upstream POP3/IMAP server doesn't  
accept or generate broken headers.
