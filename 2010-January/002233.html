On Wednesday 20 January 2010, Stefan Onken wrote:
> Hello,
>
> I am receiving some emails with incorrect header lines. Usually the
> whitespace before multiline header lines are broken (spamassassin
> messages). Fetchmail is actually dropping those emails complaining about
> an incorrect header. I googled for a solution and got stuck with a lot
> of problems but no solutions. Is there a way to force fetchmail to
> ignore those lines ?

I have the same problem. Long lines are wrapped by my mail server (Mercury/32) 
and then fetchmail refuses to deliver the mail because it is invalid. The 
rational is that fetchmail must not be responsible for propagating an invalid 
mail identified as such.

Nevertheless, I wrote a small patch to ignore those mails and pass them to the 
next hop. It was created for fetchmail version 6.3.8. I don't know if it 
still apply nicely on the newer version.
