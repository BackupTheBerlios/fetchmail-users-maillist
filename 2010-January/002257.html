>   Would the "--antispam NNN" option work as well? I did try "--antispam

antispam option only affects whether fetchmail will send a bounce or
not.

>   Is there any way to have fetchmail add a timestamp to the log during
> normal (non-verbose) operation? Earlier versions would indicate when time
> it woke and slept; v6.3.13 gives no such indication.

Currently, you will have to log to syslog for that. In your
fetchmailrc file, you will have to add:

set syslog

and check your mail logs for details with timestamps.

>   What does the entry "(log message incomplete)" imply?

That looks like a fetchmail internal message. You may safely ignore
that.
