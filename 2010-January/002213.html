As a one-time courtesy on this list, the symptom looks like improper  
configuration of Postfix, its aliases database, omitted postalias run, or  
incomplete or damaged user database.

Please check the Postfix manuals, local(8) in particular, and FAQ; try  
http://www.postfix.org/ -- if that doesn't help (though I guess it will),  
please refer to the postfix-users@ mailing list archives and mailing list  
for assistance and include the output of running "postconf -n". It's  
probably also good to include the full log (in your case, you'd grep the  
log for the queue ID, F378524FBE in that case).

Once you're starting to include fetchmail in your setup, you're welcome to  
ask again here, but please include full logs, too; see  
<http://www.fetchmail.info/fetchmail-FAQ.html#G3> for what's needed then.
