Please run fetchmail in verbose mode and check the result.

$ fetchmail -v [your other options]


Most likely, your filtering proxy is forcibly closing connection after
the spam mail. If that is the case, you may have to run fetchmail with
a batchlimit of 1 and add this to your fetchmailrc file:

poll sohnen-moe.com
  [your other options]
  batchlimit 1

If this works, please report the results.
