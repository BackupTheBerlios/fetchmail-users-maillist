On Thu, Jan 14, 2010 at 14:05, Wagner Pereira <wpereira at pop-sp.rnp.br> wrote:
> Hi, folks.
>
> My first time here. I hope I can learn a lot with you guys!
>
> Tell me someone: where does fetchmail store the messages retrieved from
> another mail server?

It doesn't, it hands it over to your local delivery mechanism (by
default the SMTP server listening on loopback).

> How can I get know if the messages were retrieved
> from it?

By checking your log files or looking in the inbox on your mail server ;)

> I've installed the TTS Request Tracker and I intend to use fetchmail to
> retrieve e-mail messages from other server to create a ticket into
> Request Tracker automatically.

Check the documentation for TTS RT and see how it tells you to
integrate email.  All you then have to do is configure fetchmail to
deliver email to the SMTP server and you're sorted.
