On Wed, Feb 11, 2009 at 11:52, Ken Gillett <ken at ukgb.net> wrote:
> I've not been able to determine from the docs, what exactly happens if
> fetchmail is run when the SMTP service is NOT available? The issue
> really concerns the start order of the various parts of the
> mailserver. If fetchmail starts retrieving messages BEFORE the SMTP
> server is running, what happens? Does fetchmail in fact wait until
> SMTP service is available, or create lots of errors or, what?

Fetchmail will attempt to contact the SMTP service, time out and then
wait for the next poll cycle before trying again.

You'll probably see errors in the logs, but no mail will be lost.
