Am 25.02.2009, 13:26 Uhr, schrieb Chris <cpollock at embarqmail.com>:


> Shouldn't I be able to telnet to the host:
>
> [chris at localhost ~]$ telnet 72.14.247.109 995
> Trying 72.14.247.109...
> Connected to ag-in-f109.google.com (72.14.247.109).
> Escape character is '^]'.
> Connection closed by foreign host.

Not for Port 995, which requires SSL negotiation.

You'd go like this:

openssl s_client -connect pop.gmail.com:995

or

gnutls-cli -p 995 pop.gmail.com

This will not run the thorough certificate checks that fetchmail performs,
but either works for me.
