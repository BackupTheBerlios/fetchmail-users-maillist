Well, from the logging, it seems that the socket gets disconnected right  
after the connection, even before SSL negotiation is attempted, since  
there is no protocol logging whatsoever and there are no complaints.

See if fetchmail -vvv changes anything in your logging, or if you can find  
out something with tcpdump or wireshark (tshark), or by running fetchmail  
under strace or truss supervision (then, the few lines around the "socket  
error" will be interesting).
