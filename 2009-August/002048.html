> I have been using fetchmail for many years to bring my E-mail,
> which accumulates at my ISP, patriot.net, back to my home computer.
> 
> Occasionally I travel, and I then use the PatriotNet web site,
> and its SquirrelMail facility, to look at some messages.  These
> messages are then marked as "Read"; and when I get home my
> fetchmail command fails to bring home these "Read" messages.
> 
> I don't know why this is, and I'd like to put a stanza/phrase in
> my .fetchmailrc which would ensure that _all_ E-mail in my
> account at PatriotNet would be brought home when fetchmail is
> called.
> 
> I have perused the documentation and can't find anything to
> correct this simple problem; hence this request for help.

I think this is what you are looking for:

man fetchmail(1):

  Disposal Options
       -a | --all | (since v6.3.3) --fetchall
              (Keyword: fetchall, since v3.0)
              Retrieve both old (seen) and new messages from the mailserver.
