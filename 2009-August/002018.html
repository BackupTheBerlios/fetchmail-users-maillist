First, read the FAQ, particularly G3 about reporting problems.  We
need your version of fetchmail (hint, if it's older than 6.3.11 you
need to upgrade) and contents of .fetchmailrc.

Note that fetchmail expects to be the only thing handling a mailbox.
You can use POP3 and the UIDL option to achieve what you're after, but
it is not currently possible with IMAP.
