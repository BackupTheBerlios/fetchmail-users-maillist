To avoid losing mail on Postfix when experimenting with configuration  
changes, use "postconf -e soft_bounce=yes", that treats all permanent  
errors as temporary (so no mail is lost).  Fetchmail 6.3.10 and newer also  
have a switch, called softbounce, that defaults to "on".
