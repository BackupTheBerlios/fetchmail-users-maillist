On Fri, Sep 18, 2009 at 14:19, Gerard <gerard at seibercom.net> wrote:
> Can fetchmail be used in conjunction with Postfix virtual MAILBOX
> separate domains, non-UNIX accounts. If anyone here has it actually
> working, ere there any problems in configuring it? The mail would be
> delivered directly to Postfix and not an MDA like procmail.

Short answer: yes.

Long answer: Fetchmail doesn't know or care how your SMTP server (and
what is beyond it) works.  It simply pulls the emails from the remote
POP3 or IMAP server(s) and hands it over for delivery.  I currently
run fetchmail on a host separate from the rest of my network - none of
the final users have accounts on it and this doesn't cause any
complications at all.
