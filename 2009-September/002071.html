Please read the FAQ about bug reporting.  At a minimum we need:

1) Version number of fetchmail
2) Contents of fetchmailrc
3) Output of "env LC_ALL=C fetchmail --nodetach -vvv --nosyslog" with
whatever other command-line options you use
4) Output of "netstat -pant|grep :25"

I assume you've checked your MTA's logs to confirm that your MTA isn't
rejecting the connection?
