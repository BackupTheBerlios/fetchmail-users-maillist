Two questions:

1) Why not use daemon mode?
2) Why not use cron?

You seem to be trying to do something the hard way ;)

I'd also suggest you don't mix command line options and configuration
files.  Put all your settings into the configuration file.  Mixing the
2 can make troubleshooting more complicated later.

Finally, the ultimate question has to be - what are you trying to achieve?
