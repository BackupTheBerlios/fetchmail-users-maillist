> Hi.
>
> I'm trying to configure Fetchmail to deliver via LMTP instead of SMTP.
> It's actually configured with SMTP and I never had any problem.
>
> On a single account, I added options
>  smtphost localhost/7025 lmtp
>
> but when fetching, I get the following error:
>
<---SNIP--->
> #************fetchmail: message my at recipient@pop3.server.it:51 was not the expected length (1216 actual != 1182 expected)
> fetchmail: LMTP>. (EOM)
>
>
> I tried adding "forcecr" but no change.
>
> What could it be?

The warning relates to the message received *from* the POP3 server.
It's telling you that the message received was longer than the POP3
server said.  There's no problem since it's just a warning and if you
check the log you'll probably find that the message is correctly
processed otherwise.
