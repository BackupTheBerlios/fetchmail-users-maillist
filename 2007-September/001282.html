system cannot offer, by definition.

Yes, that would have been helpful :-) This enterprise stuff usually sees
support times beyond 18 months (60 months are common, not sure about
NOES).

> Until OES2 becomes non-beta, this will have to remain as is.  Too many

Basically support for any distribution that the current maintainers do
not use is always tier-2 and dependent on end user and packager help.
"basically SUSE 9.x" means not "identical to SUSE 9.x".

Personally, I have access to Ubuntu 7.04, openSUSE 10.2, FreeBSD
(currently 6.2-STABLE), I may have an Ubuntu 6.06LTS image lying on some
computer where and I can occasionally run compile tests on Solaris 8,
NetBSD-whatever-version-the-kind-soul-runs and sometimes even MacOS X,
but the latter three depend on machines I have no control over.
