I've found GMail a significantly better service than Hotmail ever was.

service I've not had any problems.

If they're breaking the RFCs then by all means report the problems to
them (and document them in a blog/create threads on digg or
slashdot/whatever).  However even in the case that spawned this
thread, I'm not sure they're technically breaking the RFCs, just POLA
