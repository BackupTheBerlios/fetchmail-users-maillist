On Mon, Feb 25, 2008 at 2:33 PM, Neal Becker <ndbecker2 at gmail.com> wrote:
> I'd like to use fetchmail to forward a copy of my mails to google, but I'd
>  like to gpg/mime encrypt them.  Any ideas on how to implement this?

(You may want to read the recent thread on using fetchmail to forward to gmail)

Your best best is likely to be to use a tool integrated with your MTA
(sendmail, postfix, exim, whatever) to do this.  In theory you could
use the likes of MIMEDefang+[sendmail|postfix] to do this (a quick
google turned up a page detailing how to sign emails that way, and it
shouldn't take much to tweak that to make it encrypt instead).
