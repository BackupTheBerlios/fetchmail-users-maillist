On Feb 20, 2008 2:18 AM, Seth Ladd <sethladd at gmail.com> wrote:
> Hello,
>
> I'm trying to use fetchmail to redirect or forward email from one mail
> server into my gmail account.  I am able to POP email out of my other
> email server just fine, but I'm unable to figure out how to configure
> fetchmail to send those emails into gmail.
>
> Gmail's SMTP server requires STARTTLS, which I cannot figure out how
> to configure.  My POP server does not require or use SSL or TLS.
>
> Is there a way to tell fetchmail to use STARTTLS when delivering email via SMTP?

I think your bigger concern would be that GMail also requires
authentication for the SMTP service, which I don't think fetchmail
supports.

The simplest solution is probably to install a local SMTP server of
your choice and configure it to support STARTTLS and AUTH.
