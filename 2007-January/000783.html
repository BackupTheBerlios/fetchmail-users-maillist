> The "Delivered-To" header is the one you're looking for.  You'll want
> to read the section titled "The Use and Abuse of Multidrop Mailboxes"
> and look at the qvirtual keyword for stripping out the "MFY" part.
>
> So, something like:
>
> poll pop.isp.net
>    qvirtual "MFY"
>    envelope "Delivered-To"
>    user remote with password secure to * here
>
> Should do what you're after, assuming that your local SMTP server is
> correctly configured :)
