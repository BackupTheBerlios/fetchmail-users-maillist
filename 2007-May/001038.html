suggest you've either got some very broken logging, or you're not

makes it impossible to help you - we need context!

What I/we need to see is the output of "fetchmail --nosyslog
--nodetach -vvv" for the problem email AND the entries from your exim
log (assuming you're delivering via exim) for that point in time.  We
also really need to see the contents of your .fetchmailrc (passwords
obscured).
