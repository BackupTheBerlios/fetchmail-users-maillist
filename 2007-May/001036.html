You don't need to use exim or another MTA, if you are only interested in
local mail delivery.  If so, then you may want to model your fetchmail
configuration after mine which delivers via procmail:

    poll "pop.gmail.com"
    protocol pop3
    username "${USER}@gmail.com"
    password "${PASSWORD}"
    fetchall
    ssl
    nokeep
    mda "/usr/bin/procmail -d %T"

where ${USER} and ${PASSWORD} are as appropriate.
