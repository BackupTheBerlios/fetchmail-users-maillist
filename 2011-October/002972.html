There's also another option - if, as already suggested, "pop" in this
case is a server farm, it is possible that they do some "clever" tricks
with DNS views, too.

Ed, could you try several times - and, it would be best, over several
hours or even days - to simply ping pop.homapump.com from your LAN and
see what IP addresses it returns, then do the same from outside the
network.  If it turns out that the IP address sets are different, this
could still mean problems at their side - a clever trick that was
somehow not done quite right and bit back.
