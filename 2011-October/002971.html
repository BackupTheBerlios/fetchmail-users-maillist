That narrows the problem down considerably. It suggests that the
problem lies between your LAN and the mail provider. It could be:

a) Your modem/router (some, like Cisco, are known to interfere in
certain protocols)
b) Your ISP
c) Their connectivity

If you can try from another connection using your ISP, but a different
modem/router, it will help.
