Rob, Ed,

I'd concur.  Given past experience with Courier-related stuff, it's
usually reliable. However, chances are that either:

- the POP service Ed is using has set up load balancing, and a sub set
of servers behind the balancer are malfunctioning and/or misconfigured; or

- there's a malfunction/misconfiguration/overload between Courier's
authlib/authdaemon stuff on one hand, and Courier-IMAP as the real
IMAP/POP3 server on the other hand.


Ed,

this should be taken up with your failing POP server's user support in
either case, and make it very clear that the failures is intermittent
without your changing anything on your end, particularly you're not
changing username/password.
