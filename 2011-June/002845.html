For the records:

I have received the desired configuration off-list, downloaded the
Archlinux fetchmail 6.3.19-2, glibc 2.13-5 and OpenSSL 1.0.0.d-1
packages, for both i686 and x86_64, and ran the Archlinux fetchmail
tests on a Ubuntu 11.04 x86_64 kernel 2.6.38-8-generic, and can confirm
that fetchmail sends the password exactly as specified in the rcfile.

Thus, I still can not reproduce the problem.  That doesn't mean that I
don't believe there was a problem, but just that I have no means to
debug it.  Pandu having deinstalled Archlinux, there's no way left to
debug this.

Pandu plausibly ruled out the possibility of a server fault by trying
Cygwin 6.3.18 and Archlinux 6.3.19 several times at the same time, so
that the remaining sources of the problem are either with the running
kernel or with the hardware on the computer running Archlinux.

This particular problem thus doesn't look like a fetchmail fault to me,
but still the circumstances of the fault *did* warrant reporting and
further investigation.

Let me remind you that all problem reports should always include the
information requested in
<http://www.fetchmail.info/fetchmail-FAQ.html#G3>, and that submitters
should be prepared to answer further questions as to the bug.
