Dear Peter,
 
I am using fetchmail 6.3.9 rc2 to retrieve email from catchall account ( main at example.com) and then distributed to user1 at example.com,user2 at example.com, etc,
 
Now i am using envelope "Envelope-to". All email from TO dan CC are okay.
 
The problem is that fetchmail cannot fetch header from BCC and mailing list email.
 
And then I analyzed the header, and i found that all email from BCC and mailing list email flagged with "X-Apparently-To".
 
How can fetchmail read two header, so if header envelope-to are not found then it's automatically found header X-Apparently-To? 
 
Or there is another way?
 
Here is my fetchmailrc config :
 
poll pop.example.com with protocol POP3, with options uidl
localdomains example.com
no dns
envelope "Envelope-to"
user main at example.com with pass "secret" to * here
with options rewrite fetchall
smtpaddress example.com
 
 
Thx :)
