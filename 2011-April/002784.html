>
> I'd suggest you talk to your mail provider and ask them to fix their
> broken setup. Their mail server should only be using a single header,

would be easy to deal with by renaming it to the first if the first is
absent. procmail could do this easily, a script based on gawk shouldn't
be too hard either. The big question is where in the chain does one best
put such a filter script? I don't immediately see a mechanism where
fetchmail filters the downloaded email through another command.
