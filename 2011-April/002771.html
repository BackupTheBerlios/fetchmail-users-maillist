Well, thanks to everyone's help, it is all working correctly now. It
might be worthwhile to make a notation in the "man" pages that the use
of "localdomains' and changing the 'envelope' setting to
"Delivered-To" might/are required to make it work with "plus
addressing". In the case of at least Postfix, the use of
"Dropdelivered" is required to prevent a mail loop and its subsequently
being dropped by Postfix.
