Hary,

What you want is a so-called backport of fetchmail to Debian Lenny;
in Debian-speak, a "backport" is a package that is taken from the current
testing distribution and is rebuilt for a stable release, either the current
stable release (at present Squeeze) or a prior one (in your case, Lenny).
AFAICS, there's no "official" backport of any versions of fetchmail more
recent than 6.3.9 for Lenny; I'll try to build one today so you can try it.
