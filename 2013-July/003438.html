A 400 series SMTP error is a temporary error, the softbounce setting
controls the behaviour for permanent errors.

The safest option is to log on to Gmail and remove the offending emails.
You could configure fetchmail to permanently delete emails on a 451 error,
however if you have any DNS issues then you'll potentially discard all your
new emails
