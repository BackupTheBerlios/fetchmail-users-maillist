Chances are that Ubuntu instructions allude to Ubuntu-specific add-ons
that you do not have in Linux-From-Scratch setups.

I discourage running as root, so your $HOME/.fetchmailrc is the right
way to do it.  You can start fetchmail from cron, possibly with a
"@reboot" line.  You may want to use "fetchmail -s" (possibly adding
other options) in your cron tables.
