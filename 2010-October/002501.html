Am 12.10.2010 14:58, schrieb nightduke:
> I want to check with fetchmail an email on a worst mail server, i have
> huge spam at that email but i still need use it.
> 
> Can i check email with fechtmail and check spam and clean spam mail on
> that email and keep the headers of good emails untouched?

Yes, you can. How you do that depends on how you intend to fetch. You can:

- have fetchmail forward into your regular mail transfer agent (often Sendmail,
Postfix, Exim) and install spam/virus scanners there - this is the default and
requires an SMTP server listening on localhost port 25.

- have fetchmail forward into a mail delivery agent (formerly procmail, now
discouraged, or maildrop, recommended) and configure that to feed your mail
through spam/virus filters before delivering it to your mailbox. See the --mda
option.
