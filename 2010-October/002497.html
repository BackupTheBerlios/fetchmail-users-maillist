Fetchmail will thus look for multidrop addresses that end in
@pop.secureserver.net (from poll), @secureserver.net (from aka), and those
addresses listed explicitly (there are none in your rcfile on the left-hand
side). In either of the two domains, fetchmail will recognize and rewrite jeff
and jklotz.

You can specify full mail addresses, such as:

to jeff at mydogbosco.com=jmckinzie at jeffmckinzie.com ...

However, since there is no "aka mydogbosco.com" or thereabouts, no luck for you
on these addresses:

> About to rewrite To: jeff at mydogbosco.com
> Rewritten version is To: jeff at mydogbosco.com
> 
> *fetchmail: no local matches, forwarding to postmaster
...

> About to rewrite To: user1 at mydogbosco.com
> Rewritten version is To: user1 at mydogbosco.com

Add "aka mydogbosco.com" to your rcfile and retry.
