Have you tried including the header
Mail-Followup-To: fetchmail-users at lists.berlios.de
or getting you MUA to do it automatically?  I find this works nine times
out of ten.  With Mutt, which I see you are using, once you have set
"subscribe" in .muttrc to include the various lists you subscribe to,
Mutt will include this header whenever you do a group reply to the list.

Of course there will always be people who will reply directly and there
are (still) buggy MUAs which just plain get it wrong. :-(
