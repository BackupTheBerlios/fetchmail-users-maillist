On Mon, Jul 21, 2008 at 10:39:29AM -0600, Robert Dodier wrote:
> Hello,
> 
> I am trying to retrieve mail from a POP3 server.
> I have many folders there. I can retrieve mail from my inbox
> successfully. I also want to get the mail in the folders.
> However when I try the --folder option on the command line,
> I get an error message, "Option --remote is not supported with POP3".
> Hmm. What shall I do to get mail from POP3 folders?
> I guess moving all the messages to the inbox is a solution
> but I was hoping for something more convenient.
> 
> Thanks for any info.

POP3 has no idea of "folders" and can only ever retrieve your INBOX.
fetchmail supports some proprietary folder extensions (Check the
manual), if none fit your purpose, try IMAP - but note that fetchmail
assumes it's the only client ever accessing your IMAP mailbox.
