Phillip Susi wrote:
> N.J. Mann wrote:
> > Have you tried including the header
> > Mail-Followup-To: fetchmail-users at lists.berlios.de
> > or getting you MUA to do it automatically?  I find this works nine
> > times out of ten.  With Mutt, which I see you are using, once you
> > have set "subscribe" in .muttrc to include the various lists you
> > subscribe to, Mutt will include this header whenever you do a group
> > reply to the list.
> >
> > Of course there will always be people who will reply directly and
> > there are (still) buggy MUAs which just plain get it wrong. :-(
>
> Interesting... I can find no documentation on that header anywhere, but
> it seems to work; Thunderbird correctly replies to only the list for
> reply-to-all, but to you directly for reply.

It's actually from a long-expired internet draft inspired by Usenet.  
Never became official, but it's widely supported in free software.  A 
copy can be found here:
<http://www.ietf.org/proceedings/98dec/I-D/draft-ietf-drums-mail-followup-to-00.txt>

A bit more here:
  http://cr.yp.to/proto/replyto.html
