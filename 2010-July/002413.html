On the license, I figured that GNU GPLv3 was out, but I was totally  
unaware we've had a link in our FAQ that might have changed to the new  
version. I've now updated the online FAQ to point to and mention v2 (or  
later) and updated the link. Some parts have different licenses though.   
The authoritative information is in the file "COPYING" in the fetchmail  
tarballs. The current version (which may not be newer than the lastest  
tarball) is currently at  
<http://gitorious.org/fetchmail/fetchmail/blobs/master/COPYING>.

Note that - to the best of my knowledge - Eric S. Raymond is not keeping  
the catb.org-sited fetchmail information in synch with the canonical  
version, which is currently at  
<http://www.fetchmail.info/fetchmail-FAQ.html#G1>.
